Hi there! I'm curious about the history of Nordic design. Can you tell me more?

LLM

Certainly! Nordic design, also known as Scandinavian design, emerged in the early 20th century, gaining prominence in the 1950s. It's characterized by simplicity, minimalism, and functionality, often incorporating natural materials like wood, leather, and wool. Key figures include Alvar Aalto, Arne Jacobsen, and Hans Wegner, whose work emphasized democratic design principles, making beautiful and functional objects accessible to all.
